About BlueDucks Limited

Why Choose BlueDucks?

BlueDucks Limited combines cutting-edge technology expertise with proven industry experience. Our team delivers high-quality software solutions while maintaining a commitment to excellence in both development and education. We don't just build software - we build careers through our internationally recognized academy.

Our headquarters is strategically located At No. 10 Ajali Cresent, Independence layout, Enugu, Nigeria, serving clients across Africa and internationally. We leverage our local presence to understand regional needs while maintaining global standards in software development and training.

We offer comprehensive training in programming languages, web development, mobile app development, networking (CCNA), game development, and multimedia design. Our students receive hands-on experience, internship opportunities, and career placement support with industry-leading companies.

Our core services include custom software development, mobile application development, web design and development, UI/UX design, animation and multimedia services, video editing, application management, and comprehensive tech education with internationally recognized certification.
